This is the **Pooled Usage** documentation – it explains how Enterprise teams can use a **shared usage pool** instead of fixed per-user allocations.

Think of this as the **flexible budgeting model** for Enterprise – everyone draws from a single committed amount, so usage is available wherever it's needed most.

Let me break this down.

---

## What Is Pooled Usage?

**Pooled usage gives your Enterprise team a shared usage pool instead of fixed per-user allocations.** Everyone draws from a single committed amount that spans your contract period, so agent usage is available wherever it's needed most.

| Without Pooled Usage (Teams) | With Pooled Usage (Enterprise) |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Each user gets their own $20/month | Shared pool across all team members |
| Unused capacity can't be redirected | Capacity available where needed |
| Per-user caps | Single committed budget |
| Month-to-month tracking | Cumulative across contract term |

---

## Why Pooled Usage Matters

> *"Without pooled usage, unused capacity from team members can't be redirected to those doing the most demanding work. Pooled usage removes that constraint, letting your full budget serve your team's highest-priority projects."*

### The Problem Without Pooled Usage:

| User | Allocated | Usage | Status |
|------|-----------|-------|--------|
| Developer A | $20 | $15 | $5 unused |
| Developer B (critical project) | $20 | $25 | ❌ Hits cap, can't get more |

### With Pooled Usage:

| Total Pool | Usage | Status |
|------------|-------|--------|
| $100 (5 users × $20) | $80 total | ✅ Available where needed |

---

## Fewer Interruptions

> *"Without pooled usage, a developer working on a critical project can hit their cap while other allocations sit unused. With a shared pool, the team's committed budget is available to whoever needs it."*

**Result:** Critical work isn't blocked by artificial per-user limits.

## How It Works

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Your team commits to a total usage amount for your contract period |
| 2 | All team members share this pool |
| 3 | Usage is tracked **cumulatively across the contract term** (not month by month) |
| 4 | Dynamic spend limits and alerts provide ongoing governance |
| 5 | Shared pool remains the primary budget constraint |

## Common Beginner Questions

### Q: Is pooled usage available on Teams plans?
**A:** No – pooled usage is only available on Enterprise plans.

### Q: How is usage tracked with pooled usage?
**A:** Cumulatively across the full contract term, not resetting monthly.

### Q: Can unused budget be rolled over?
**A:** With cumulative tracking, the committed amount spans the contract period.

### Q: Can I get alerts when usage is high?
**A:** Yes – Spend Alerts notify you at configurable thresholds.

### Q: Do spend limits scale with team size?
**A:** Yes – Dynamic Spend Limits automatically scale as your team grows or shrinks.

### Q: Who can adjust pooled usage settings?
**A:** Only admins can adjust pooled usage settings.
